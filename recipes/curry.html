Chicken Curry

Description

Authentic chicken curry. Delicious and easy.

Ingredients

  • 800g Chicken thighs
  • 1 large onion - grated
  • 8 cloves of garlic - roughly chopped
  • 4 tsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 5cm stick of cinnamon
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 400g can of chopped tomatoes
  • 50g ginger - roughly chopped
  • 250ml hot chicken stock
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan on a low heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds, fennel seeds, cinnamon stick, and chilli flakes. Wait until fragrant.
  3. Add grated onion and fry until thickened.
  4. Add garlic and ginger. Stir continuously until fragrant.
  5. Add garam masala, turmeric and sugar and stir for 30 seconds.
  6. Add can of tomatoes and let simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Add chicken and cook through.
  8. Pour over hot chicken stock.
  9. Add salt and pepper if desired.
  10. Garnish with coriander and serve.
