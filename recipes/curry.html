Chicken Curry

Description

Authentic chicken curry. Delicious and easy.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cumin
  • Turmeric
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Grate onion into paste
  2. Fry onion until thickened
  3. Add garlic and cook until fragrant
  4. Add chicken and cook through
  5. Add turmeric and Cumin
